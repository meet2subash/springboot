EXPEDITED GROUND

Forward Air supplies expedited ground transportation between our terminals located near major U.S. and Canadian airports. Placement of our stations and sort centers provide us with the ability to transport freight within our network in a more expedited fashion than traditional LTL ground transportation. This aiport-to-airport (A2A) service is provided to domestic and international airlines, freight forwarders and related logistics businesses. Our network is your network.

DEDICATED LINEHAUL

Forward Air Truckload is the full truckload arm of our LTL division. We provide full truckload service for larger point-to-point consignments and dedicated fleet services. With Forward Air Truckload, you have the option to ship LTL, TL and/or integrate specialized cartage services. Truckload offers a diversified portfolio of transportation services (long haul, regional and local truckload capacity) and a wide assortment of equipment that enables us to safely and quickly meet critical on-time expectations across North America.

FINAL-MILE SERVICES

Complete® Cartage extends your reach farther into areas outside of our airport locations by adding pickup/delivery to our linehaul service as a packaged arrangement. This integrated, end-to-end (or “final mile”) service offers forwarders, airlines, logistics companies and non-traditional shippers, reliable, single-source transportation to zip/postal codes and zones across North America. Use our network as your own to enhance your business scope and enlarge your customer base. Options available within our Complete® offering includes to-the-door, local zone transport, distributions, residential, and much more.
